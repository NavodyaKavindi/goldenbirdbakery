About us

Golden bird confectionary was started on 06.08.2002 as an individual company. This company engaging manufactures and distribute bakery products. In the beginning, we have only 12 members as a workforce. Now we have increased our production capacity. Presently we have more than 150 employees. On 13.02.2017 we registered our company as Golden bird bakers and confectionary private limited. The managing director of our company is Mr.Don Sarath Samarakoon. Our address is Golden bird confectionary(pvt) LTD, No,75/A, Yagodamulla, kotugoda, Sri Lanka.


We manufacture our products under the guidance of health authorities and their regulations. Therefore, we have obtained A standard bakery products manufacturing company and also we have been able to get a G M P quality standard certificate. We want to improve our distribution network and also further improvements of quality standard product manufacturing.
